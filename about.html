My Story

Hi there! My name is Tyler Rathod, and I'm a native of the Bay Area. I'm currently in my junior year studying Information Systems at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo.

Career

I started my journey into product design as a web developer, interning professionally as a frontend engineer and junior web developer before pivoting towards a career in product design.

Now, as a Management of Information Systems major, I work at the intersection of product design, development, and management. While getting a formal education in Information Systems and developing product management skills like competitive analysis, user research, and data synthesis, I have worked independently to develop my product and UX/UI design skills as well.

What I Add

My extensive background and continued work in web development, combined with my formal education in MIS and well-honed design skills allow me to design products with the user, business, and developers in mind.
